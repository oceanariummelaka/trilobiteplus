Trilobite Fact


Trilobites were present on Earth for more than 300 million years. This shows just how successful they were at adapting and evolving.
In comparison, our ancestors have been around for only six million years, with modern humans only appearing about 200,000 years ago.
That means that trilobites were around for more than 50,000 times longer than modern humans have been on the planet!
